What can you do with a degree in Biology?

Biology can be defined as the study of life and living organisms. It encompasses a wide range of topics, including the molecular-level study of groups of organisms and their interactions within ecosystems and with other living organisms. A Biology degree opens doors to a variety of career opportunities. Careers in biology are mainly focused on studying living organisms to broaden the understanding of biological processes aiming to discover new treatments for diseases and contribute to environmental conservation. Many biology graduates often choose to pursue postgraduate studies to specialize in a specific field, while others pursue careers in different fields including healthcare, microbiology, biotechnology, and forensic science, or continue further studies in medical school.
