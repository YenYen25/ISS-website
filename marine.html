MARINE BIOLOGIST

Marine biologists focus on studying marine life including organisms and ecosystems found in oceans. They investigate the behavioral patterns and physiology of marine organisms, examine the conditions of marine habitats, and assess the impact of human activities on marine life. heir research often includes conducting species inventories, monitoring and testing sea creatures exposed to pollutants, collecting and analyzing ocean samples, preserving specimens of unknown species and diseases, and movements of marine populations.

Salary: $55,487 - $111k

Working hours: 35 hours per week
