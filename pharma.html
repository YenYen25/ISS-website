PHARMACOLOGIST

Pharmacologists are the scientists involved in developing new medications to tackle diseases. They examine the effects of different drugs and chemicals on living organisms in order to ensure their effectiveness and safety. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), the job outlook for pharmacologists is expected to grow by 6% from 2019 to 2029.

Salary: $108,952 - $182,975

Working hours: 40 hours per week
