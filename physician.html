PHYSICIAN

A physician is commonly known as a medical doctor who diagnoses and provides treatment for diseases and injuries. They are responsible for examining patients, prescribing medications, maintaining the medical histories of patients and disease control and prevention. Physicians can also specialize in different fields of medicine including psychiatry, pediatrics, dermatology, cardiology, and anesthesiology.

Salary: $106,543- $355,368

Working hours: 50 hours per week
