MICROBIOLOGIST

Microbiologists focus on studying microorganisms including bacteria, viruses, fungi, algae, archaea, and protozoa with the aim of addressing a wide range of issues related to health, environment, food, and climate. They are involved in the development of new medicinal drugs, vaccines, hormones, vitamins, enzymes, and pharmaceuticals. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), the job outlook for microbiologists is expected to grow by 5% from 2022-2032.

Salary: $48,030 - $106,490

Working hours: 40 hours per week
