FORENSIC SCIENTIST

Forensic scientists examine and analyze evidence from crime scenes. They use scientific tests to analyze materials such as body hairs, nails, pieces of clothing, tyre marks, blood, and other body fluids. Forensic scientists typically specialize in a specific area of forensics. Such subspecialties include forensic pathology, forensic toxicology, cyber forensics, and forensic psychology. As stated by the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) the field of forensic science is expected to grow by 14% by 2028.

Salary: $49,085 - $103,613

Working hours: 39 hours per week
