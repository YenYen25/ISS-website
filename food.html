FOOD SCIENTIST

Agricultural or food scientists specialize in research to develop methods to improve food production, safety, and quality. They focus on various aspects of agriculture and food systems to enhance crop yields, develop better farming practices, and ensure the safety and nutritional quality of food products. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), the job outlook for nanotechnologists is expected to grow by 10% in the next 10 years.

Salary: $40,520 - $125,280

Working hours: 40 hours per week
